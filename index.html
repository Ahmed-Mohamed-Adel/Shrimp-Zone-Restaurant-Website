About Us

Shrimp Zone is a seafood restaurant known for its delicious and fresh seafood offerings, with a special focus on shrimp. The restaurant prides itself on using high-quality ingredients and serving a variety of seafood dishes, from perfectly grilled shrimp to seafood platters that cater to every taste. With a commitment to providing an exceptional dining experience, Shrimp Zone has become a popular destination for seafood lovers, offering both mouthwatering dishes and a welcoming, friendly atmosphere. The restaurant is owned by Hany Food, an Egyptian blogger known for his passion for food and culinary experiences. Hany’s love for seafood and his expertise in the food industry have shaped the restaurant’s unique concept. Under his leadership, Shrimp Zone continues to grow and delight customers with its authentic flavors and excellent service. Hany’s vision is to provide a memorable dining experience that keeps customers coming back for more, ensuring that each visit is as satisfying as the last.
